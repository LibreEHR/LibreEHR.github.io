About

LibreEHR is forked from OpenEMR (an ONC certified open source electronic medical records and practice management application) to create invovation through diversity; we aim to continue the spirit of openness, kindness, and cooperation.

Our goal is to take the inspiration of OpenEMR (an open-source, platform-agnostic, application, conceived in 1998) and bring the technologies & application standards up to date, creating a robust alternative to proprietary counterparts.

Our objective is to create a truly modular EHR system, utilizing well-designed standalone and 3rd-party components, to aid in the longevity and progress of this project and many physicians offices.
